--- Body ---

Care coaches are human participants who prepare for and lead coaching sessions.
They coordinate schedules with clients, review readiness materials, and run
live sessions. They are distinct from the Busy coach persona, which describes
research-backed motivations — this actor is the concrete graph participant.
