--- Body ---

Care coaches need a reliable way to create and update coaching sessions without
back-and-forth email coordination. This capability covers the core scheduling
flow: selecting participants, choosing a time, and confirming session details.

When a coach schedules a session, the system should validate that required
participant information is present before confirmation. The capability touches
the Session domain concept and supports the Session scheduling workflow.
