--- Body ---

Coaches currently lose time to manual session coordination — confirming times,
chasing participant details, and reconciling calendar conflicts. This
objective prioritizes reducing that overhead so coaches can focus on session
preparation and delivery.

The Session scheduling workflow operationalizes this objective. Progress is
observable through the Less manual scheduling expected result.
